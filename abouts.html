About Us

Welcome to The Sanjalika Water World, Karachi, a truly world class water by all means. Sanjalika Water World is a hi-tech, action packed, thrilling entertainer with amazing features for all ages. Visitors bursting with energy, The Sanjalika can be yours ultimate water sports holiday destination. At The Sanjalika Family Water Park, you can invite families to join you for an action packed summer holiday destination.

For those who just want to relax and unwind it is cool-cool indoors where you’ll find a wealth of things to do, from zipping down our scintillating slides to amazing rides. You can do as much or as little as you like in a safe environment for you and your family.
